info About the Artist

Brightsin is a rapper and music artist from Nkomazi, Mpumalanga — a small town that shaped his voice before the world had a chance to hear it. After completing his matric in 2016, he channelled his energy into music, finding in it both a creative outlet and a form of self-expression rooted in where he came from.

Driven by a hunger to grow beyond his craft, Brightsin enrolled at the Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) in 2019, pursuing a qualification in Information Technology. He balanced studio sessions with lectures, refusing to let either pursuit slip. In October 2024 he graduated — walking off the stage with a degree in one hand and a debut EP in the works with the other.

His debut EP Out of My Shell (2025) marks the formal introduction of Brightsin to the world — a project built over years of lived experience, self-discovery, and grind.
