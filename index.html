info About the Artist

Brightsin is a rapper and music artist from Nkomazi, Mpumalanga — a small town that shaped his voice before the world had a chance to hear it. Music was always in him; even as a youngin he always knew that one day he would make music. That calling became reality in 2017, when he officially started making music and channelled everything he had into the craft.

Driven by a hunger to grow beyond music alone, Brightsin enrolled at the Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) in 2019, pursuing a qualification in Information Technology. He balanced studio sessions with lectures, refusing to let either slip. In October 2024 he graduated — walking off stage with a degree in one hand and a debut EP in the works with the other.

His debut EP Out of My Shell (2025) marks the formal introduction of Brightsin to the world — a project built over years of lived experience, self-discovery, and grind.
